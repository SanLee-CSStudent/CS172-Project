About Our Residential Colleges

Our residential colleges engage students in a living and learning environment which is designed for making precious memories, pride-worthy grades, long-lasting friendships, and engaged citizens. With faculty and upperclassmen living alongside students, they serve as an academic example and guide for students to achieve their goals and make their home away from home as comfortable as possible.
