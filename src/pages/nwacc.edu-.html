NWACC Presidential Search 

NWACC has begun the search for a new college president, who will follow in the footsteps of Dr. Evelyn E. Jorgenson after she retires on June 30, 2022. The new NWACC president will continue the college's tradition of delivering accessible, affordable and high-quality education. Learn more about the search process and updates on the NWACC Presidential Search website.
