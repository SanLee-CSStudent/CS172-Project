Central Arizona College to Recognize Graduates

May 3, 2022
Central Arizona College will hold two Graduation Ceremonies to recognize this years college graduates. Commencement ceremonies will take place at 7 p.m. on May 12 and 7 p.m. on May 13, 2022.

Central Arizona College Offering Free Tuition For Pinal County and Arizona Residents This Summer

April 28, 2022
Central Arizona College will continue to offer free in-state tuition to Pinal County residents for the Summer 2022 semester. Additionally, all Arizona residents who do not reside in Pinal County will receive six free credits of in-state tuition.

CAC Names Pence Music Room After Rick Moore, Former Professor and Band Director

April 27, 2022
During the recent spring community band concert, Central Arizona College officially dedicated room 149 in the Don P. Pence Center for Visual & Performing Arts as the Rick Moore Music Rehearsal Room.
