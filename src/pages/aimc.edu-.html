Your journey to an inspiring career in healing begins here.

We invite you to pursue your calling and put your unique fingerprint on acupuncture, health, and healing. We use Traditional Chinese Medicine as the foundation of our teaching, and integrate the best of biomedicine, Japanese, French, and five-element approaches to diagnosis and treatment. At Acupuncture & Integrative Medicine College, Berkeley (AIMC) you can bring your unique perspective to the world of today’s medicine, find your specialty, and create a successful practice.
