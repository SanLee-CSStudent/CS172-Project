Transfer Students

Congratulations on your admission to UCA! We recognize you have had previous college experience and want to make sure your transition to UCA is smooth. TRANSFERmation is an optional orientation program geared towards incoming transfer students the semester you transfer to UCA.? It is designed to showcase resources and opportunities you will find integral to your success while at UCA.

TRANSFERmation will be held the day before instruction begins each fall and spring semester.?To register, please complete the registration form below.?Family members are welcome to attend with you.

  • For spring 2022, TRANSFERmation will be held on Wednesday, January 12th?in the?Student Center Ballroom. The program will be live streamed for students choosing to attend virtually. Registration will close Monday, January 10.
  • For fall 2022, TRANSFERmation will be held on Wednesday, August 24th in the Student Center Ballroom. Registration will open Wednesday, June 1st.
  • For spring 2023, TRANSFERmation will be held on Wednesday, January 11th in the Student Center Ballroom. Registration will open Tuesday, November 1st.

We look forward to meeting you!
