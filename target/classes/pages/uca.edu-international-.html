Our mission

Welcome to the new Center for Global Learning and Engagement at the University of Central Arkansas (formerly International Engagement).

Our new name reflects our commitment to engaging the entire UCA community in the many opportunities for global learning available at UCA. The Center for Global Learning and Engagement (CGLE) at the University of Central Arkansas works to promote intercultural understanding and to enrich the academic and cultural environment of the University by creating programs to promote the exchange of people and ideas.?

The Center for Global Learning and Engagement will continue to recruit, welcome, and support international students and scholars as well as all those seeking educational opportunities through eduication abroad and National Student Exchange.

CGLE brings a world of learning to UCA. Our programs prepare all students to live well in local and global communities.?

CGLE will continue to house the Offices of Education Abroad and National Student Exchange, International Student and Scholar Services, International Admissions and Recruitment, and the new Center for Chinese Language and Culture.

As in the past we will maintain our close relationship with the Intensive English Program.?
