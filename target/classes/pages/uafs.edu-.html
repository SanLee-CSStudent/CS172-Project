Your Future is Our Focus

Our priority at UAFS is preparing students like you for success. We offer more than 100 future-focused degrees.

Business Administration

The BBA offers concentrations in management, finance, economics, marketing, and accounting.

Nursing

The BSN program prepares nurses for a variety of levels. Opportunities abound in this exciting and fulfilling profession.

Elementary Education

There are numerous career opportunities for individuals who graduate with a degree in elementary education.

Studio Art

The UAFS studio art program provides students advanced study in drawing, painting, printmaking, and digital photography.

Biology

The Bachelor of Science in biology program is delivered by highly qualified and dedicated faculty.

Logistics Operations

The Bachelor of Applied Science in logistics operations is designed for industrial and corporate professionals.
