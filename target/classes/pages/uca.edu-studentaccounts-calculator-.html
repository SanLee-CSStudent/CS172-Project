Tuition and Fees Calculator




 

Disclaimer: This tool provides only an estimate of the basic charges involved in tuition. The calculation does not reflect any deduction of financial aid and/or scholarships.

Additional Fees Not Included: Application Fee, Housing Deposit (Normally $100; Deposit Not required for Summer), Greek Village Fee ($225/semester), Course & Lab Fees, Honors College Participation Fee, PO Box Fee, and International Admin & Ins. fees are in addition and not included for this estimate.

May Intersession housing is only available to spring residents. August Intersession housing is only available to fall residents.

Note: To qualify for Undergraduate Fully Online rate, you need to be admitted through the UCA Online program.

Note: For a list of eligible Fully Online graduate programs, refer to the Selected Online Programs with Special Tuition and Fees document.

Refer to the UCA Out-of-State Fee Waiver Information page for additional information.
