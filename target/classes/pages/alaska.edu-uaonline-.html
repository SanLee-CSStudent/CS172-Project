University of Alaska response to the Covid-19 virus:

For information concerning the Covid-19 virus from the University of Alaska, please refer to the University of Alaska coronavirus information page.

The information on that page will be updated, as necessary, as the situation changes.

Students and Employees:

Select direct deposit for refunds and reimbursements today! 

Direct Deposits are prioritized and processed daily. Checks are printed less frequently. To receive your refund or other reimbursement payments as quickly as possible and avoid mailing delays, enroll in Direct Deposit.  Go to the Student Services & Account Information tab for the link.

Employees

Direct Deposit for Payroll is available through an electronic form. 

Students

Some official notifications will be sent to your preferred email address while others will be sent only to your UA email address.

To set or update your preferred email address, visit the UAOnline Personal Information tab and click Manage Your Address, Email and Phone Information.
