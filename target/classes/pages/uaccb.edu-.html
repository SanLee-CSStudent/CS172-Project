Admissions Checklist
Ready to get started? Complete your application for admissions online or visit our admissions office on campus. We are ready to help.
01 | Test Scores
UACCB accepts ACT, SAT, Compass, Asset and Accuplacer scores taken within the last five years.
02 | Transcript
The high school transcript requirement can be waived if the student transfers 12 or more hours from a previous college.
03 | Immunization
UACCB requires proof of at least two MMR immunizations (MMR I & MMR II) upon admission to the school.
