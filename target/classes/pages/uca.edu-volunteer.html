Get Involved in Service

UCA’s broad and diverse network of local, regional, national, and international community partners offers a variety of volunteer opportunities for students. How will you get involved?

Check?CubConnect?for a calendar of volunteer events, or go directly to GivePulse, our service tracking tool, to see our list of community partners (“Affiliates”) and contact them directly for opportunities to get involved.?Call 501-852-7416 or email volunteer@uca.edu if you need?help connecting with an organization or finding a?project?that fits your interests and schedule.

Receive emails about upcoming volunteer opportunities:?Click Here?to join our monthly mailing list!

 

?Take the Bears Serve Challenge!

  • SERVE the community through UCA-sponsored opportunities or those you find on your own
  • TRACK your service hours on GivePulse (Find instructions here!)
  • BE RECOGNIZED?for completing the BearsServe Challenge (30 hours in an academic year) or the Community Engagement and Service pathway on the BearX Co-Curricular Transcript!
  • Ready to graduate? Request a community service graduation cord if you’ve logged at least 100 hours during your time at UCA!

Tell us how YOU are serving throughout the year using #BearsServe on Twitter and Facebook!

Connect with UCA Volunteers!

Are you a community organization who needs volunteers??Email us to request an invitation to affiliate with us on GivePulse, where you can share your events with UCA students and the public, manage shifts and volunteers, and track the impact of volunteers on your organization! Learn more about the resources available on the Community Partner Resource Page and email us to get started.

If you have questions or need more information, please contact volunteer@uca.edu.
