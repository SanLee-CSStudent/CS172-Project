Veteran Services

The UCA Veteran Resource Center is open Monday through Friday from 8:00am – 4:30pm. For assistance, please email us at veteranservices-a@uca.edu.?All emails should include: The Student’s Name, Chapter Number, Student ID, Phone Number, and Issue in Detail. Emails are responded to daily. Please allow up to 24-hours for a response.?Stay safe and healthy!

 

Mission Statement:?The UCA Veteran Resource Center’s mission is to help veterans, current service members, and dependents utilize their VA Benefits.

 

 

Our Services Include:
  • Certification of VA Benefits
  • Information about Veteran Affair benefits
  • Information about scholarships available to military affiliates
  • NGTW Assistance
  • Help to complete scholarship applications
  • Connections to campus services

 


For questions toward the UCA Certifying Official please call 501-852-2999 or email veteranservices@uca.edu??The UCA Veteran Resource Center operates on a “first-come, first-served” policy. Alternatively, if you have a question regarding the status of your paperwork, updates concerning our office, or general questions, email us at veteranservices-a@uca.edu.

The UCA Veteran Resource Center’s hours of operation are 8:00am to 4:30pm, Monday through Friday.
